# Quick Start Guide for Israel Survey

Welcome to the National Survey of Perioperative Anesthesia Practices in Israel. This guide will help you get started with the survey.

## Steps to Participate
1. **Eligibility:** Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria for participation.
2. **Sign Up:** Register through our official link.
3. **Preparation:** Review the guidelines and required documents.
4. **Survey Completion:** Follow the instructions to complete the survey.
5. **Submission:** Submit your responses by the deadline.
